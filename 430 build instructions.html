430 build instructions


1.Install system fan with 2 screws
2.Install power supply (EN-PS-101) with 4 screws (2 in back, 2 underneath)
3.Install Zotac backplate (EN-BP-009) with 2 screws
4.Install 16 channel DVR card backplate (EN-BP-007) with 2 screws
5.Install 4TB 3.5 7200RPM desk star (EN-DC-102) with 4 screws
6.Attach Red data cable left angle to hard drive
7.Install Zotac H87ITX motherboard (EN-MB-106) with 2 screws(back)
8.Install 1840 processor (EN-CP-106) with triangle matching to motherboard
9.Install CPU fan (EN-BL-100) push 4 tabs until they click install cable fan to motherboard
10.Install ATX black yellow fuse cable (power cable for analog cameras)with 2 rounds screws (EN-CB-105)
11.Install 16 channel DVR card (EN-DV-103) to PCIE connector (EN-RC-102) Zip tie together
12.Install 16 channel DVR card (EN-DV-103) andPCIE connector (EN-RC-102) onto motherboard push down onto motherboard screw tight with 2 round hex screws
13.Connect power supply (24pin connector) cable to motherboard
14.Connect system fan cable to motherboard
15.Attach Sata power supply cable to hard drive
16.Attach black yellow ATX cable to power supply
17.Use zip ties to tie off any loose cables or wires(follow cabling routing instructions)
18.Install lid with 4 screws
19.Attach 430 sticker to front panel
20.Attach wan / cam lam sticker to back of unit
