420 build instructions

1.Install power supply (EN-PS-101) with (4) screws into chassis screw down
2.Install system fan screw down front of chassis with (2) screws
3.Install H87ITX back plate (EN-BP-009) with (2) screws
4.Install H87ITX motherboard EN-MB-106) onto chassis with (2) screws
5.Install Intel Celeron 1840 processor (EN-CP-108) match triangle clamp down
6.Install CPU fan (EN-BL-100) onto motherboard push 4 tabs until they click
7.Install 4TB 3.5 7200 RPM desk star hard drive (EN-DC-102) with (4)screws
8.Install 4 GB RAM (EN-ME-100) onto motherboard slot
9.Attach power cable (24 pin) to motherboard,
10.Attach Sata Cable from hard drive to motherboard
11.Attach system fan onto motherboard,
12.Attach sata power cable to hard drive

13.Attach 420 logo sticker (EN-ST-008) to front of chassis
14.Attach Wan Cam Lan sticker (EN-ST-102) to back port of chassis
15.Follow cabling routing instructions
16.Attach lid with four screws
17.Finished unit
